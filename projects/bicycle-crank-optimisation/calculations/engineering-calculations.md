# Engineering Calculations

## Pedal force

The design load assumes the full weight of a 70 kg rider acts on one pedal.

\[
F = mg
\]

\[
F = 70 \times 9.81 = 686.7\ \text{N}
\]

## Crank moment

Using a crank lever arm of 0.17 m:

\[
M = Fd
\]

\[
M = 686.7 \times 0.17 = 116.739\ \text{N m}
\]

## Allowable stress

The selected material is Aluminium 7075 with a yield strength of 498 MPa. For the required minimum factor of safety of 2:

\[
\sigma_{\text{allowable}} = \frac{\sigma_y}{2}
\]

\[
\sigma_{\text{allowable}} = \frac{498}{2} = 249\ \text{MPa}
\]

## Optimised-design factor of safety

\[
FoS = \frac{\sigma_y}{\sigma_{\max}}
\]

\[
FoS = \frac{498}{48.027} = 10.37
\]
